Chocolate Banana Protein Smoothie

Description

One of my favourite smoothies to make, really the only one that I make. I got tired of buying smoothies from the gym and figured that making them at home as regularly as I was purchasing them would pay for itself in a week or two.

The fibre content will vary, but the taste and protein more than make up for it.

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana, fresh or frozen, ripe or overripe. It is your choice
  • 1 Scoop Chocolate Protein Powder
  • 300-500ml Coconut Water
  • 1 heaping scoop Peanut Butter
  • 1 heaping scoop Nutella
  • Ice as desired

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Additional ice or coconut water can be added to modulate thickness as desired
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy!
