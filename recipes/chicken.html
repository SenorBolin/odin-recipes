Spiced Chicken

Description

A staple in the household, perfect for mealprep for the busy modern family

My partner and I love to cook up a minimum of 8 servings on a Sunday arvo, ready for a weeks worth of lunches

Ingredients

The below will be measured out for a single serving, thankfully most of the measurements are vibes and dreams

  • Chicken Breast, 250g
  • Frozen Mixed Vegetables
  • 1/3 cup white rice (cooked)
  • All Spice Seasoning
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Moroccan Seasoning
  • Paprika Seasoning

Steps

  1. The day before you plan to cook, dice the chicken to your desired size.
  2. Apply seasonings to the chicken and let it mariinate overnight.
  3. On the day of, cook the rice as required, I prefer to have this set before starting anything else
  4. While the rice is cooking, portion out your frozen veggies, I usually aim for 100-150g per serving.
  5. Cook chicken in a pan until sufficiently cooked through out all pieces. Use the larger chunks to check
